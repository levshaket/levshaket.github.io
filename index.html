dreidelware.org

Dreidelware: generate secure and memorable passphrases on web clients.

  • window.crypto is used as the cryptographically strong source of pseudo random integers that serve as indices to memorable wordlist provided to your device. The passphrase generation is conducted on your device. This means that if you believe you may have malware on your device, please do not use this application.

  • Please do not spin dreidels in real life (or coins, for that matter) to obtain random numbers. Spinning is a poor randomization process, since it amplifies the minute density differentials of the spinning object. This means that the nature of every dreidel game depends on the dreidel used.

  • |S| = P(2**16, n), where S is the search space and n is the desired passphrase length. For n=4, |S| > 1.8E19, acceptable in 2019. Adjust n (number of dreidel rows) according to your passphrase complexity needs.
